What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application collects the following information to provide and improve our services:

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

We share limited information with the following third parties:

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

What are my opt-out rights?

You have several options to limit data collection:

  • Uninstall the app to stop all data collection and delete local data
  • Don't sign in to Google Play Games to avoid sharing profile information
  • Don't use online multiplayer to avoid Firebase data collection
  • Adjust your device's ad personalization settings to limit advertising data
  • Use airplane mode to play offline and avoid most data sharing
Data Retention: Online game data (Firebase) is automatically deleted after 24 hours. For Google Play Games and advertising data, please use Google's privacy controls.
